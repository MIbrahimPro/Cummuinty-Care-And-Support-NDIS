Our Services

Comprehensive NDIS support tailored to your needs.

In-Home Care

Personal care, domestic assistance, and daily living support in the comfort of your home.

Community Access

Building confidence and skills to participate in social and community activities.

Nursing Care

Specialized clinical and nursing support delivered by our registered professionals.

Supported Living (SIL)

24/7 support in a shared living environment to foster independence.

Support Coordination

Helping you navigate the NDIS, connect with providers, and manage your plan.

Transport Services

Reliable travel and transport assistance for appointments and community outings.

Life Skills Development

Personalized training in areas like cooking, budgeting, and travel.

Respite & STA

Short Term Accommodation and assistance to give carers and participants a break.

Household Tasks

Assistance with cleaning, gardening, and maintaining a safe home environment.

Therapeutic Supports

Access to a range of allied health professionals to support your wellbeing.

What to Expect

Joining CCAS means joining a family. We prioritize open communication and consistency.

  • Personalized Matching: We match you with workers who share your interests.
  • Regular Reviews: We check in often to ensure your plan evolves with you.
  • Transparent Costs: No hidden fees, strictly NDIS price guide compliant.
